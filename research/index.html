Research

Academic Statistics

I am currently working on two thesis projects on model testing in functional data methods. In the first project, we propose a Bootstrap-based procedure for testing covariance in longitudinal data, with specific application to testing Linear Mixed Effects (LME) models. I presented preliminary results at the 2017 Joint Statistical Meetings in Balitmore, MD. In the second project, we develop a model testing procedure for binary scalar-on-function data.

Applied Statistics

I am currently a Graduate Industrial Trainee with the Population Bioequivalence (PBE) of IPAC-RS. We are studying the performance characteristics of the PBE criterion for evaluating in-vitro data for inhaled products. A paper covering application to delivered dose measurement from metered dose inhalers has just been submitted, and a technical report is available online for IPAC-RS members.
